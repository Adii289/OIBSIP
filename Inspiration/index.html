My Inspiration: A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," has always been a source of motivation for me. His journey from a small town boy to the President of India is a true example of hard work, humility, and dedication.

What I Adore

I admire his simplicity, honesty, and vision for a developed India. He believed in the power of youth and often said, "Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."

His speeches and books, especially "Wings of Fire", have inspired millions of students, including me, to believe in ourselves and contribute positively to society.
